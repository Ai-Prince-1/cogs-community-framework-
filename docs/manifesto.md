# The COGS Manifesto

Version 0.5

## Why COGS Exists

Modern society suffers from increasing loneliness,
declining civic trust,
social fragmentation,
and weakening local communities.

COGS exists to provide a practical,
open-source framework for helping people grow,
support one another,
and build resilient communities.

## What COGS Is

COGS is:

- a community operating system
- an open-source framework
- a practical protocol for human flourishing
- a decentralized network of circles

## What COGS Is Not

COGS is not:

- a religion
- a political movement
- a self-help organization
- a centralized institution
- a belief system

## Core Principles

- Practice over belief
- Truth over ideology
- Service over status
- Stewardship over ownership
- Community over tribalism
- Learning over certainty
