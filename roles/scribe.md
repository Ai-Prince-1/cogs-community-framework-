# Scribe Playbook

**Your job in one sentence:** Keep the record alive and accessible.

---

## At Each Gathering

**Take brief notes on:**
- Key topics covered
- Decisions made
- Commitments stated
- Tensions or breakthroughs
- Any changes to the structure or agreements

**Note-taking style:**
- Short, simple language
- Quote key moments (especially from retrospectives)
- Capture action items and owners
- Don't transcribe everything — capture the essence

---

## After Each Gathering

- **Publish notes to the group thread within 48 hours**
- Use a simple format:
  ```
  **[Date] Gathering Notes**
  - Topic 1: Description
  - Topic 2: Description
  - Commitments: [list]
  - Next Facilitator: [Name]
  ```
- Make sure notes are accessible to all members at all times
- Don't overthink it — clear and honest is enough

---

## Maintain the Season Record

**Keep organized records of:**

1. **Season Opening**
   - Domains chosen
   - Personal intentions (each member's)
   - Facilitator rotation order
   - Resource decision
   - Outward support decision

2. **Weekly Commitments**
   - A running log of what members committed to each week
   - What they reported back on
   - Patterns or themes that emerge

3. **Mid-Season Check-In (Week 6)**
   - What shifted?
   - What adjustments were made?
   - Energy assessment

4. **Season Retrospective (Week 12)**
   - All eight retrospective questions and answers
   - Themes and learnings
   - Role rotation for next Season
   - Decision: continue, pause, or close?

---

## Produce the Season Summary

**At the Season Retrospective, create a written summary that includes:**

1. **Season at a Glance**
   - Dates and domains
   - Key milestones or turning points

2. **Key Learnings**
   - What genuinely worked?
   - What didn't?
   - What did the group learn about itself?

3. **Commitments**
   - Kept / partially kept / missed (with patterns)

4. **One Improvement for Next Season**
   - What would you change?
   - Why?

5. **Role Rotation for Next Season**
   - Who's facilitating when?
   - Steward, Bridge assignments (if Level 2)

---

## Accessibility

- **The notes belong to the Circle, not to you**
- Make sure every member can access them at all times
- Store them in at least two places (shared doc + printed + cloud backup)
- Hand the complete record to your successor clearly organized

---

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ Don't transcribe everything word-for-word
- ❌ Don't edit for tone or correctness
- ❌ Don't keep notes secret or behind a password
- ❌ Don't add your own commentary or interpretation
- ❌ Don't skip documentation because "we'll remember"

---

## Handing Off to Your Successor

- Give them the complete organized record
- Walk them through the storage system
- Show them what you captured and why
- Answer questions about previous Seasons
- Remind them: clarity and honesty matter more than perfection
