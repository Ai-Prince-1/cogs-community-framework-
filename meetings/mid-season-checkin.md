# Mid-Season Check-In

A structured conversation halfway through the season to assess progress, adjust course, and maintain alignment.

## Purpose
- Reflect on progress toward season goals
- Identify what's working and what's not
- Make mid-course corrections
- Reconnect with shared purpose
- Prevent misalignment before it becomes a problem

## Agenda (90 minutes)

### Opening Circle (10 min)
- Gather and center
- Reminder of season theme/goals

### Progress Review (30 min)
- What was our intention for this season?
- What have we accomplished so far?
- What metrics or observations show our progress?
- Where have we fallen short?

### Challenges & Blockers (20 min)
- What obstacles have we encountered?
- What's harder than we expected?
- What external factors are affecting us?
- Where do we need support?

### Adjustments & Next Steps (20 min)
- What should we continue doing?
- What should we stop or change?
- What new priorities emerge?
- Who needs to take what action?

### Closing (10 min)
- Affirm shared commitment
- Set expectations for next phase
- Gratitude round

## Facilitator Notes
- Keep the tone reflective, not judgmental
- Look for patterns, not just individual incidents
- Test for consensus on proposed changes
- Document decisions and next steps
- Follow up on commitments
