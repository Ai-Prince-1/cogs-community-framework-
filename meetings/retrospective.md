# Season Retrospective

A reflective gathering at the end of a season to celebrate achievements, learn from challenges, and prepare for renewal or closure.

## Purpose
- Honor what was accomplished
- Extract learning from the experience
- Acknowledge individual and collective growth
- Decide the Circle's next chapter
- Complete the seasonal cycle

## Timing
- Held 1-2 weeks before season end
- Allows time for reflection
- Gives space for next season planning if continuing

## Agenda (120 minutes)

### Opening Circle (10 min)
- Gather and ground
- Acknowledge the journey
- Set container: honesty, generosity, curiosity

### Season Review (20 min)
- What was our season intention?
- What did we accomplish?
- What surprised us?
- What changed about us?

### Appreciation Round (20 min)
- Each person reflects on who/what they appreciated
- Go around the circle, uninterrupted
- Acknowledge specific moments or contributions
- Feel the gratitude fully

### Challenges & Learnings (20 min)
- What was difficult?
- What did we learn from failures or setbacks?
- What would we do differently?
- What needs healing or closure?

### Individual Impact (15 min)
- How did this experience change you?
- What will you take with you?
- How will you apply these learnings?

### Decision Point (15 min)
- Does this Circle continue next season?
- Do we need a break, refresh, or redesign?
- Who's committing to continue?
- What needs to shift?

### Closing Ritual (20 min)
- Gratitude practice
- Blessing or benediction
- Symbolic closure (optional: marking with a small ritual)
- Contact exchange for ongoing connection

## Facilitator Notes
- This is sacred time—protect it
- Encourage depth and honesty
- Balance celebration with critique
- Document key learnings
- Consider bringing in an outside facilitator for objectivity
- Follow up with written summary and next steps

## Output
- Written reflection on season
- List of learnings and implications
- Decision documented: continue, pause, or close
- Commitments for next phase
